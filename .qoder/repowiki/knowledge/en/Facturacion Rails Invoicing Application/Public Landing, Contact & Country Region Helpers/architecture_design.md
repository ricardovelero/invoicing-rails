Thin MVC controllers under `app/controllers/` that inherit from `ApplicationController` but opt out of authentication via `skip_before_action :authenticate_user!` (`HomeController`) or simply expose non-protected endpoints (`ContactController`, `CountriesController`). Views live in `app/views/home/` (ERB partials `_menu.html.erb`, `_mobile_menu.html.erb` plus `index`, `privacy`, `terms`) and `app/views/countries/regions.turbo_stream.erb` for Turbo Stream responses. Internationalization is split into two namespaces: `config/locales/home/` for page copy and `config/locales/routes/` for route labels; both ship English and Spanish files. The helper module `HomeHelper` exists as a placeholder with no methods yet. Dependency direction is one-way: controllers → ActiveRecord models (`Country`, `Contact`) and views → helpers + locales; nothing inside this scope depends on other application modules beyond those shared models.
